AiColorExtractor was created to help Print-on-Demand businesses, designers, and e-commerce sellers quickly generate professional color palettes for better branding and marketing. How to Use AiColorExtractor / FAQ

Step 1: Click the '+ Add Images' button and upload any image you want to extract colors from.

Step 2: Wait a few seconds while AiColorExtractor analyzes the image and generates a color palette.

Step 3: Click on any color box to copy its HEX code to your clipboard.

Step 4: Use the extracted colors in your POD designs, marketing materials, or branding projects.

FAQ: AiColorExtractor works on all modern browsers and does not require downloads. All data processing occurs locally for privacy.
