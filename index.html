About AiColorExtractor

AiColorExtractor is an advanced online tool that makes it easy to identify and copy HEX and RGB codes directly from any image. Whether you’re a designer, digital artist, or web developer, this tool saves you time and effort.

It supports all major image formats including JPG, PNG, and GIF. Extract color palettes quickly and copy them instantly for your creative projects.

Using AiColorExtractor is simple: upload an image, view the extracted colors, and click on a color to copy its code to your clipboard.

This tool is completely free, requires no registration, and works directly in your browser.

From website design to branding, AiColorExtractor helps you discover color combinations and palettes that boost your creative workflow.

How AiColorExtractor Works

AiColorExtractor uses smart algorithms to scan your uploaded image and extract the most prominent and accurate colors. Each extracted color is shown with its HEX and RGB values, ready to copy and use instantly.

  • Click “Choose File” to upload your image (JPG, PNG, GIF).
  • The tool analyzes the image and displays color swatches below.
  • Click any swatch to copy HEX and RGB codes.
  • Paste the codes into your design software or project.
