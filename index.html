How AiColorExtractor Works

AiColorExtractor helps designers, developers, and creatives to extract accurate HEX and RGB color codes from any image in seconds. Simply upload your image, and the tool will analyze its pixels and generate the dominant colors.

Supported formats include JPG, PNG, and GIF. The extracted colors can be copied instantly by clicking on the color box. This makes it perfect for web development, branding projects, and digital art.
