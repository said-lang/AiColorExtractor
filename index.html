How It Works

AiColorExtractor works by analyzing every pixel of your uploaded image and extracting the most dominant and relevant colors. It instantly generates accurate HEX and RGB codes that you can use in your design tools, POD platforms, or branding guidelines.

This online color palette generator is ideal for designers, print-on-demand entrepreneurs, digital artists, and anyone who needs to maintain visual consistency across projects. With one click, you can copy the extracted colors and use them in Photoshop, Canva, Illustrator, or your favorite design tool.

All processing happens in your browser, which means your files remain private, and no data is stored on our servers. AiColorExtractor supports popular formats like JPG, PNG, and GIF, making it accessible for a wide variety of creative projects.
